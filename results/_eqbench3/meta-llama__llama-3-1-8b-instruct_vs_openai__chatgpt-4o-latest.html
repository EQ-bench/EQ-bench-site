The deeper insight here is that emotional support is not always about offering solutions or even about protecting someone from their own dark thoughts. Sometimes it’s about entering the storm with them—not to fix, but to witness. The failure here is not one of love, but of attunement. And from that failure, both participants emerge with clearer boundaries—but also, perhaps, with a deeper understanding of what it means to truly be “on someone’s side.”
